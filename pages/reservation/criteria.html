Admin Guide: Criteria for Student Room Allocation

The Criteria Page is where administrators can define criteria for student room allocation. Each criterion includes a description (equation) and weight. When a student registers successfully, their data is checked against these criteria (evaluating the criteria equations). If a criterion matches the student's data, their score is increased by the weight of the criterion.

For numerical fields (e.g., GPA, Certification Score):
Simple Numerical: e.g., GPA > 3
Complex Numerical: e.g., 70 < Certification Score < 80
For categorical fields (e.g., Gender, Governorate):
Simple Comparison: e.g., Gender == "Male"

Scenario 1:
Student GPA: 3, Gender: Male
Criterion: GPA > 2, Weight: 4
Result: Criterion evaluates to true, increase in Total score by 4.

Scenario 2:
Student GPA: 3.5, Gender: Female
Criterion: GPA > 3, Weight: 3
Result: Criterion evaluates to true, increase in Total score by 3.

Scenario 3:
Student GPA: 2.5, Gender: Male
Criterion: GPA > 3, Weight: 3
Result: Criterion evaluates to false, no increase in Total score.
